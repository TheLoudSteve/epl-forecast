ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

How are the forecasts calculated?

Forecasts are based on each team's current points per game rate extrapolated over a full 38-game season. For example, if a team has 20 points from 10 games (2.0 PPG), their forecast would be 76 points for the full season.

How often is data updated?

The app updates data automatically once daily (12:00 AM UTC) and during live matches. You can also pull down to refresh manually.

Can I track multiple teams?

You can set one favorite team for personalized notifications and highlighting. You can change this anytime in Settings.

Why don't forecasts match other predictions?

Our forecasts use a simple points-per-game calculation rather than complex algorithms. This provides a clear, data-driven projection based purely on current performance.

How do I enable notifications?

Go to Settings > Notifications and configure your preferences. You can choose immediate or end-of-day notifications and set sensitivity levels.
